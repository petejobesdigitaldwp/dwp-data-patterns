"Manhattan" Employer Information Service

This service lets you view information about employers.

It is currently derived from HMRC Pay-As-You-Earn (PAYE) data, from the 2015/2016 tax year, broken down by Local Authority District.

Use this tool to find information about:

  • The number of employees living in a location by employer
  • Employer salaries by location
  • Growth or contraction of an employers workforce living in a location (this measure is experimental)
  • The geographic spread of employers and employees
  • Filterable visualisations and tables
